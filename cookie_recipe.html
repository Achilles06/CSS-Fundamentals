Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed light-brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet and/or milk chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the flour and baking soda; set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the butter with both sugars; beat on medium speed until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the salt, vanilla, and eggs and beat until well mixed.
  5. Gradually add the flour mixture; mix until just combined.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop by rounded tablespoon onto baking sheets lined with parchment paper.
  8. Bake until cookies are golden around the edges, but still soft in the center, 8 to 10 minutes.
  9. Remove from oven, and let cool on baking sheet 1 to 2 minutes.
  10. Transfer to a wire rack, and let cool completely.
